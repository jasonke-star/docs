## What is PartGenie?

PartGenie is an AI-driven platform designed specifically for engineers, R&D teams, and procurement professionals. Our mission is to transform the tedious and time-consuming component selection workflow into an intelligent, efficient one-stop process. We're not just a search engine, but your dedicated AI electronic engineering assistant, capable of transforming complex technical problems into complete and executable solutions.

## Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Hardware R&D Engineers / Design Engineers">
- Quickly and accurately find and compare components
- Validate design feasibility
- Get in-depth interpretation of datasheets
- Find alternatives to optimize design or avoid risks
- Component selection in early design phases
- Component upgrades/replacements during product iterations
- Solve compatibility or performance issues in designs
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Electronic Procurement Managers / Supply Chain Professionals">
  - Manage BOMs 
  - Get real-time pricing and inventory information 
  - Monitor component lifecycle (EOL/NRND) 
  - Find cost-optimized or more stable supply alternatives 
  - Conduct supplier evaluations 
  - BOM cost analysis and optimization 
  - Supply chain risk early warning and management 
  - Develop procurement strategies 
  - Assist in supplier negotiations
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="FAE / Sales Engineers">
  - Quickly respond to customer technical inquiries 
  - Provide solutions or product recommendations 
  - Conduct competitive analysis and highlight product advantages 
  - Access latest technical materials and application cases 
  - Technical preparation before customer visits 
  - On-site technical problem solving for customers 
  - Provide customized solutions
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Hardware Entrepreneurs / Students / Hobbyists">
- Quickly learn component knowledge
- Find low-cost, easily accessible components for prototype development
- Perform basic BOM management
- Product prototype design
- Learn electronics knowledge
- Project material list management
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

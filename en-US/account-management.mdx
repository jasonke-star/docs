<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I modify my personal information?">
  **Modification Steps:**
  1. Click avatar in bottom left → "Settings"
  2. In the personal profile page, modify:
     - User nickname
     - User avatar
     - Identity settings (helps AI provide personalized recommendations)
     - Industry settings (helps AI provide personalized recommendations)
  3. Click "Save" button to confirm changes
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I change my email?">
  **Change Steps:** 
  1. Click avatar in bottom left → "Settings"   
  2. In account & password page, modify email 
  3. Click "Bind Email" button next to email 
  4. Enter new email and check email for verification code to verify identity 
  5. Check email and click verification link to complete email binding
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I unbind my email?">
  **Unbinding Steps:** 
  1. Click avatar in bottom left → "Settings" 
  2. Select account & password page 
  3. Click "Unbind Email" 
  4. Check email for verification code to verify identity 
  5. Confirm unbinding 
  6. Unbinding successful
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I change my password?">
  **Change Steps:** 
  1. Click avatar in bottom left → "Settings" 
  2. Select account & password page 
  3. Click "Set Password" 
  4. Enter current password for verification 
  5. Enter new password and confirm 
  6. Save settings
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I bind Google?">
  **Binding Steps:** 
  1. Click avatar in bottom left → "Settings" 
  2. Select account & password page 
  3. Click "Bind Google" 
  4. Use Google login to authorize 
  5. Confirm successful binding
  **Notes:** 
  - One Google can only bind to one account 
  - After binding, you can use Google for quick login 
  - If you need to unbind, please refer to unbinding operations
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I forgot my password?">
  Please contact customer service: support@partgenie.ai
</Accordion>

  Device management functionality is being planned, please stay tuned for future updates
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

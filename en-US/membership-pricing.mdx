## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to choose the right membership plan?">
  Choose based on your usage needs:
  - Individual users: Free or Pro plan
  - Team/Enterprise: Team/Enterprise plan (demo scheduling supported)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">
  Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime, effective from the next billing
  cycle
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
  Currently only supports Stripe payment
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to apply for an invoice?">
  After payment completion, apply for invoice in Personal Center -> Membership
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Team/Enterprise version priced?">
  Team/Enterprise version uses custom pricing. We recommend scheduling a demo
  first to understand product features, then contact our sales team for detailed
  quotes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to schedule a product demo?">
  You can schedule a demo through the following methods:
  - Online booking form: https://partgenie.ai/book-demo
  - Contact sales email: support@partgenie.ai
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you support refunds?">
  **Refund Policy:** - Virtual value-added services, no refunds once sold -
  Please carefully understand product features before purchase
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to apply for an invoice?">
    **Application Steps:**
    1. After payment is completed, go to Personal Center
    2. Click the "Membership" tab to view your billing information
    3. You can download the invoice in the "Invoice" tab
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

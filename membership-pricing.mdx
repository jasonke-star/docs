## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How to choose the right membership plan?">
  Choose based on your usage needs:
  - Individual users: Free or Pro version
  - Team/Enterprise: Team/Enterprise version (demo scheduling supported)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel my subscription anytime?">
  Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime, effective from the next billing
  cycle
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
  Currently only supports WeChat Pay
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to apply for an invoice?">
  After payment completion, apply for invoice in Personal Center -> Membership
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How is Team/Enterprise version priced?">
  Team/Enterprise version uses custom pricing. We recommend scheduling a demo
  first to understand product features, then contact our sales team for detailed
  quotes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to schedule a product demo?">
  You can schedule a demo through the following methods: - Online booking form -
  Contact sales email: support@partgenie.ai - Call dedicated hotline:
  +1-800-PARTGENIE
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you support refunds?">
  **Refund Policy:** - Virtual value-added services, no refunds once sold -
  Please carefully understand product features before purchase - Recommend
  trying the free version first **Free Trial:** - Free version provides basic
  feature experience, monthly AI quota auto-updates - No time limit, can be used
  long-term - Recommend full experience before considering upgrade
  **Team/Enterprise Version:** - Supports demo scheduling to understand features
  - Decide whether to purchase after demo
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How to apply for an invoice?">
  **Application Steps:**
  1. After payment completion, enter Personal Center
  2. Click "Membership" page
  3. Find "Apply for Invoice" option
  4. Fill in invoice information
  5. Submit application
  
  **Notes:**
  - Invoice processing within 3-5 business days after application
  - Supports personal and corporate invoices
  - Contact customer service if you have any issues
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

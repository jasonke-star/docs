## Quick Start

<Steps>
<Step title="Register Account">
  Visit [partgenie.ai](https://partgenie.ai) on desktop and click the "Login" button

  {" "}
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/login.png" alt="PartGenie Login Interface" />
  </Frame>

  <Info>
  You can register and login using phone verification, WeChat QR code, or email binding
  </Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete Personal Profile">
  After logging in, please complete your personal profile information:
  - **Position Information**: Select your main role (e.g., Engineer, Distributor, Procurement, Maker, etc.)
  - **Professional Field**: Choose your main work area (e.g., Consumer Electronics, Automotive Electronics, Industrial Control, etc.)

<Tip>Completing your profile helps us provide more accurate recommendations</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Perform Your First Search">
  Try searching for a component you're familiar with:
  
  ```
  Example search terms:
  - "STM32F103C8T6 microcontroller"
  - "100uF 16V electrolytic capacitor"
  - "LM358 operational amplifier"
  ```
  <Frame>
  <img src="/images/recommend-query.png" alt="PartGenie Recommended Queries" />
  </Frame>

  <Tip>You can also choose from the recommended search queries below the input box and modify them</Tip>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Search Tips

### Natural Language Search

PartGenie supports various electronic industry task queries. You can use everyday language to describe your needs:

<CodeGroup>
```text Precise Search
"LM7805CT"
```

```text Basic Search
"Need a 3.3V LDO voltage regulator"
```

```text Parameter Search
"STM32 series microcontroller, ARM Cortex-M4, at least 512KB Flash"
```

```text Application Search
"MOSFET for motor control, N-channel, 60V and above"
```

```text Alternative Search
"Domestic alternatives/substitutes for STM32F103C8T6"
```

```text Cross Reference/Comparison
"Query MMBT3904 SOT23 specifications and competing manufacturer models"
```

```text Technical Q&A
"What tests are included in AEC-Q100 standard? What reliability test standards are included?"
```
</CodeGroup>
